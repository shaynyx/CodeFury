Online Harassment Help

Block and Report

Block and report harassers on social media platforms. Reporting mechanisms are often available to notify platform administrators of abusive behavior.

Maintain Privacy

Protect personal information by adjusting privacy settings and limiting what is shared online.

Community Reporting

Encourage bystanders to report harassment when they witness it, helping to hold harassers accountable.

Legal Recourse

In some cases, harassment may cross legal boundaries, and you can seek legal remedies such as restraining orders or filing reports with law enforcement.

Cyber Crime Reporting Portal

You can report cybercrimes through the Ministry of Home Affairs' National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal This portal allows you to report various types of cybercrimes, including online harassment.

CERT-In

CERT-In is the national agency for responding to computer security incidents in India. You can contact them for guidance and reporting cyber threats.
